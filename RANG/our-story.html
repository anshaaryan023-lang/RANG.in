OUR STORY

From the heart of Mithila to the digital world — a journey of art, culture, and empowered artists.

The Origin

Rang.in was born with a single vision — to preserve the timeless heritage of Madhubani art and connect it with the modern world. Bihar is not just a place, it is a treasure trove of centuries-old art forms that tell stories of mythology, nature, and culture.

But in today's digital age, our local artists were being left behind. We saw how machine-made fake paintings were being sold in the market as originals. That's why we created Bihari Store — a platform that bridges tradition with technology, ensuring every artwork is authentic and every artist is empowered.

The Mission

Our mission is to empower local artists, ensure fair recognition, and create a trusted marketplace where every artwork tells a story of culture, tradition, and identity. Each piece is curated, verified, and celebrated.

We believe in preserving Bihar's artistic legacy while giving artists the tools and platform they need to reach a global audience.

The Vision

We envision a future where every Madhubani artist has a global audience, sustainable income, and pride in their heritage. Rang.in is not just a marketplace — it is a cultural movement.

Our goal is to expand beyond Madhubani and showcase all of Bihar's rich artistic traditions — from Tikuli art to bamboo crafts, from terracotta to traditional textiles.
