ABOUT RANG

Founder of rang.in — Ansh Raj

Namaste!

I am Ansh, and \Rang started with a small dream – a dream to make the whole world recognize the hard work of artists from every village in Bihar. Bihar is not just a place, it is a treasure trove of centuries-old art. But in today's digital era, our local artists are being left behind. I saw how machine-made fake paintings were being sold in the market as original. That's why, we created Bihari Store.
